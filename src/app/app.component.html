ABOUT PROJECT CONNECT
Project Connect is mapping the connectivity of every school in the world. We aim to connect all schools to the Internet and all young people to information, opportunity and choice.
Thank you for contributing to Project Connect. Find your school and learn more at our website. We add new schools every day!
